Teaching Aids

Interactive tools and visual aids to help understand mathematical concepts.

Trigonometry Connector

This tool allows students to see how the trigonometric functions can be visualized by looking at the unit circle and the cartesian plane with theta being the x axis. A student can go to the math center during operational hours to look at it and explore the connection. There is also a video describing how to use this item in the videos page.
