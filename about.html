About Elemental Math

Elemental Math is designed to support College Math and STEM learners with clear, fundamental-focused explanations. We also wish to make some visualization tools for the classroom and the math center to aid with understanding.

This was created for the Showker Prize Project in the 2025-2026 school year by Jacob Fields, Isaac Plange, and Bethany Hinshaw at Bridgewater College (The name of the website is the first letters of our names with "BC" thrown in)

What makes Elemental Math helpful

  • Fundamental-focused: Don't learn about logarithms without knowing what an inverse is!
  • Visual tools and teaching aids allow better understanding
  • Video walkthroughs by BC students to healp outside of class learning
  • Accessible to all students
